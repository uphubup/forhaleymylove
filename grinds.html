Coffee Grinds

The grind size of your coffee beans dramatically affects the flavor and extraction. Here's a guide to the main grind types and their uses.

Coarse Grind

Chunky, distinct particles. Best for French press and cold brew, allowing for slow extraction and bold flavors.

Medium Grind

Similar to sand in texture. Ideal for drip coffee makers and siphon brewers, balancing extraction and clarity.

Fine Grind

Powdery and smooth. Used for espresso and AeroPress, enabling quick extraction and intense flavor.

Extra Fine Grind

Almost flour-like. Perfect for Turkish coffee, producing a strong, rich cup.
